Our Mission

At Midlands State University Library, our mission is to support the academic success and research needs of our university community by providing access to a diverse range of information resources, services, and technologies. We strive to foster an inclusive and collaborative environment that encourages learning, innovation, and personal growth.

Our Vision

We envision a library that serves as a vibrant hub of knowledge, inspiring students and faculty to explore, discover, and create. By leveraging technology and cultivating partnerships, we aim to enhance the learning experience and contribute to the advancement of knowledge.

Our Services

  • Resource Access: We offer a wide array of resources, including books, journals, databases, and digital media.
  • Research Support: Our skilled librarians provide guidance in research methodologies and information literacy.
  • Study Spaces: The library features a variety of study environments, accommodating individual and group study needs.
  • Workshops and Events: We host regular workshops and events designed to enhance research skills.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of librarians and support staff is committed to providing exceptional service and fostering a welcoming atmosphere.

Get Involved

We encourage feedback and participation from our university community. Join us for events, utilize our resources, or reach out with any questions or suggestions.
